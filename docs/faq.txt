ABC Technologies - FAQ Document

GENERAL QUESTIONS:

Q: What is ABC Technologies?
A: ABC Technologies is a SaaS company providing cloud-based business management software to help organizations streamline operations, manage teams, and analyze data efficiently.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on Starter and Professional plans. No credit card required.

Q: Can I change my plan later?
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the Billing section in your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are GDPR compliant and conduct regular security audits.

BILLING QUESTIONS:

Q: How do I get an invoice?
A: Invoices are automatically emailed after each payment. You can also download them from Settings > Billing > Invoice History.

Q: What happens if my payment fails?
A: We will retry the payment 3 times over 7 days. You will receive email notifications. After 3 failures, your account may be suspended temporarily.

Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Yes. Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase per our refund policy. Annual subscription refunds require supervisor review.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Cancellations take effect at end of billing cycle. Supervisor review is required for annual plans.

ACCOUNT QUESTIONS:

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox. Links expire in 15 minutes.

Q: How do I update my profile information?
A: Go to Settings > Profile > Edit Profile. You can update your name, email, phone, and profile picture.

Q: How do I activate or deactivate a team member's account?
A: Go to Settings > Team > Members. Click the three-dot menu next to the member's name and select Activate or Deactivate.

Q: How do I close my account permanently?
A: Account closure requests must be submitted through Support and require supervisor approval. Data is retained for 30 days post-closure.

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS:

Q: Which browsers are supported?
A: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Edge 90+, and Safari 14+ are fully supported.

Q: What is the maximum file upload size?
A: The maximum file size per upload is 50 MB. Supported formats include PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PNG, JPG, and CSV.

Q: The application is crashing. What should I do?
A: Clear your browser cache, try a different browser, check file size limits, and disable conflicting extensions. If the issue persists, contact Technical Support.

Q: How do I integrate with third-party tools?
A: Go to Settings > Integrations. We support Slack, Salesforce, Zapier, Google Workspace, and more. API documentation is available in the Developer section.
