ABC Technologies - Company Policy Document

REFUND POLICY:
- Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Refunds after 30 days are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
- Annual subscription refunds require supervisor approval.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Customers can cancel their subscription at any time.
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- Subscription cancellation requests must be reviewed by a human supervisor.
- Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation.

ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY:
- Account closure permanently deletes all customer data.
- Account closure requests require supervisor approval.
- Customers receive an email confirmation before closure.
- The process takes up to 48 hours.

COMPENSATION POLICY:
- Compensation is offered for verified service outages exceeding 4 hours.
- Compensation requests must be approved by a human supervisor.
- Compensation may be in the form of account credits or subscription extensions.

ESCALATION POLICY:
- Customers may request escalation to management at any time.
- Escalations are reviewed by the support supervisor.
- A response is provided within 24 hours of escalation.

GENERAL SUPPORT POLICY:
- Support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST.
- Average response time is under 4 hours for standard queries.
- Critical issues are prioritized and addressed within 1 hour.
- All customer data is handled in compliance with GDPR and applicable data laws.
